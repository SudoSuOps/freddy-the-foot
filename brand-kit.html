05

Humor doctrine — this section is law

A little smile that leaves a real reminder. The laugh lands first; the lesson arrives about two seconds later.

1Laugh with, never at, never down. Target the life — 3am alarms, dad shoes — never the outcome.
2Punchline first, reminder as aftertaste. The reminder rides the end card, not the dialogue.
3Specificity is the comedy engine. "A LEGO at 3am" beats "check your shoes."
4The habit is background radiation — the setting of the joke, not the punchline.
5Self-aware, never self-pitying. Grumble = relatable. Despair = scroll.
6The 24/7 truth stays quiet but present — one line max. It's the dignity layer, not the content.
BANNED WORDS — NEVER ON ANY ASSET
amputation · ulcer(say "sore") · necrosis · "you could lose" · "diabetic complications" · "suffer from"(say "living with" / "managing") · any pity framing
